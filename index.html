About BADAWY TV

BADAWY TV is an Islamic Dawah platform dedicated to spreading authentic Islamic knowledge through modern technology. Our mission is to make the teachings of Islam accessible to everyone through live lectures, Quran recitation, educational programs, and community engagement.

Through our online Islamic Academy, live broadcasts, and educational resources, we aim to help Muslims strengthen their faith, increase their knowledge, and stay connected with the Quran and Sunnah.

The platform is closely connected with Masjid Aqswa Kisauni, serving the community with Islamic guidance, answering religious questions, and supporting Islamic learning for people of all ages.
