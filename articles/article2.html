The Virtues of the Quran

The Quran is the final revelation sent by Allah to guide humanity.

It is a source of mercy, healing, and guidance for those who believe.

Reciting the Quran brings tranquility to the heart and multiplies rewards for the believer.

Every Muslim should strive to read, understand, and live by the teachings of the Quran.
