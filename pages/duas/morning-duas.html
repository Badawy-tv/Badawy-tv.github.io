Morning Duas

اللّهُـمَّ بِكَ أَصْـبَحْنا وَبِكَ أَمْسَيْنا وَبِكَ نَحْيا وَبِكَ نَمُوتُ وَإِلَيْكَ النُّشُور

Allahumma bika asbahna wa bika amsayna wa bika nahya wa bika namut wa ilayka an-nushur.

O Allah, by You we enter the morning and by You we enter the evening, by You we live and by You we die and to You is the resurrection.

Reference: Abu Dawud 5068
