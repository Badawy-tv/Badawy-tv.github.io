Understanding Tawheed

Tawheed is the foundation of Islam. It means affirming the absolute Oneness of Allah in His Lordship, His right to be worshipped, and His Names and Attributes.

Categories of Tawheed

  • Tawheed ar-Rububiyyah: Believing Allah alone is the Creator and Sustainer.
  • Tawheed al-Uluhiyyah: Directing all acts of worship to Allah alone.
  • Tawheed al-Asma wa Sifat: Affirming Allah’s Names and Attributes without distortion or denial.

Importance

All Prophets were sent with the message of Tawheed. Associating partners with Allah (shirk) is the gravest sin in Islam.
