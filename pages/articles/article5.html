Islamic Knowledge Article 5

Islam teaches guidance for every aspect of life. Knowledge strengthens faith and brings the believer closer to Allah.

The Prophet ﷺ said: "Seeking knowledge is obligatory upon every Muslim."

Through authentic knowledge we understand Tawheed, worship Allah properly, and follow the Sunnah of the Messenger ﷺ.
