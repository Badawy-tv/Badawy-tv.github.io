Foundations of the Muslim Family

The Importance of Family in Islam

The family is the cornerstone of a Muslim society. It is the place where the seeds of faith, character, and knowledge are planted in the hearts of children.

In Islam, family life is a sacred and vital aspect of our lives. The relationship between husband and wife, parents and children is founded on mutual love, mercy, and respect.

Marriage as the Foundation

Marriage is not just a social contract, but a sacred bond between two people. The Islamic teachings emphasize the importance of building a family based on Tawheed (the oneness of Allah) and love for each other.
