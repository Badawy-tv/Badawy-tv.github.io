How to Pray Correctly (Salah)

By BADAWY TV • February 2026

Read More →

Salah is the second pillar of Islam and was made obligatory during the Night Journey (Isra and Mi’raj). Muslims pray five times daily: Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha. Each prayer includes specific units (rak‘ah) and recitation of Surah Al-Fatihah in every unit. Proper prayer requires purification (wudu), facing the Qiblah, and observing humility and concentration (khushu’).

Understanding Tawheed

By BADAWY TV • February 2026

Read More →

Tawheed means affirming the Oneness of Allah in Lordship (Rububiyyah), Worship (Uluhiyyah), and His Names and Attributes (Asma wa Sifat). It is the core message of all Prophets. Associating partners with Allah (shirk) is the gravest sin in Islam.

Common Misconceptions in Islam

Read More →

Islam promotes justice, mercy, and balance. The Qur’an emphasizes that there is no compulsion in religion (Qur’an 2:256). Misconceptions often arise from cultural practices or misinformation rather than authentic Islamic teachings.
