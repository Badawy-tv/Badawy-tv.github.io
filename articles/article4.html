The Value of Patience (Sabr)

Patience is one of the most praised qualities in Islam.

Allah promises immense rewards for those who remain patient during hardships.

Sabr helps believers remain firm in faith, avoid despair, and trust the wisdom of Allah.

Every challenge a believer faces is an opportunity to strengthen faith and grow spiritually.
