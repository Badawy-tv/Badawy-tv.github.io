The Power of Charity (Sadaqah)

Charity is a powerful act of worship in Islam that purifies wealth and the heart.

Helping those in need strengthens compassion and builds stronger communities.

The Prophet ﷺ said that charity does not decrease wealth but rather increases blessings.

Even small acts of kindness and giving are considered charity in Islam.
