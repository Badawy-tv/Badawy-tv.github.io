After Waking

Arabic:

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُور

Translation:

All praise is for Allah who gave us life after causing us to die.

Reference: Bukhari 6312
