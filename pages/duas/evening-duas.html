Evening Duas

Arabic:

اللّهُـمَّ بِكَ أَمْسَيْنا وَبِكَ أَصْـبَحْنا وَبِكَ نَحْيا وَبِكَ نَمُوتُ وَإِلَيْكَ المَصير

Translation:

O Allah, by You we enter the evening and by You we enter the morning, by You we live and by You we die and to You is the return.

Reference: Tirmidhi 3391
