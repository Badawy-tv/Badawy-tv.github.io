Islamic Knowledge Article 28

Islam is a religion built upon faith, knowledge, and righteous actions. A Muslim strives to improve his relationship with Allah through prayer, good character, and constant remembrance of the Creator.

Seeking knowledge is one of the greatest acts of worship in Islam. The Prophet ﷺ taught that the scholars are the inheritors of the Prophets. Learning the Qur'an, understanding the Sunnah, and reflecting on the message of Islam helps strengthen faith and guide one's life.

Every believer should aim to purify the heart, increase sincerity, and practice patience. Through sincere worship, repentance, and good deeds, a Muslim moves closer to Allah and prepares for the eternal life in the Hereafter.
