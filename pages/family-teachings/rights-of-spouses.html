Rights of Spouses

Understanding marital rights and responsibilities to maintain harmony.

Quranic Guidance

Reference: Quran 2:228

Men and women have rights over each other, maintain harmony and fairness.

Practical Examples

Example: Equitable division of responsibilities, active listening, and patience in disagreements.

Illustrative Story

Story: Observing mutual rights led to a harmonious household, admired by neighbors.
