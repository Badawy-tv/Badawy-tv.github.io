tajweed Course - Lesson 1

Lesson Summary

This lesson introduces the science of Tajweed, which teaches Muslims how to recite the Quran correctly as it was revealed.

Key Points

  • Tajweed means improving Quran recitation
  • It protects the pronunciation of Quranic words
  • Every Muslim should learn the basics of Tajweed
