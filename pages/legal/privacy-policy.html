Privacy Policy – Badawy TV

Effective Date: 2026

Badawy TV is committed to protecting the privacy and trust of visitors who use our website and educational resources. This Privacy Policy explains in detail how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when individuals access Badawy TV services including Quran reading resources, Islamic lectures, family teachings, live Islamic broadcasts, and the Ask Imam guidance platform.

1. Introduction

Badawy TV is an Islamic educational initiative dedicated to spreading authentic knowledge derived from the Qur’an and the Sunnah. Through our website and online platforms we provide lectures, Islamic guidance, Quran recitation tools, and educational materials designed to strengthen faith and increase understanding of Islam.

This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors, students, viewers, and individuals who interact with the Badawy TV website.

2. Information We Collect

Badawy TV collects limited information only when it is voluntarily provided by visitors. This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Questions submitted through the Ask Imam form
  • Messages sent through contact forms

Visitors may choose to provide this information when asking Islamic questions, requesting guidance, contacting the platform, or interacting with certain educational features.

3. Ask Imam Feature

The Ask Imam feature allows individuals to submit religious questions to qualified Islamic scholars associated with Badawy TV. When submitting a question, the visitor may voluntarily provide personal information including their name, email address, and phone number.

This information is used exclusively to allow the Imam or authorized Badawy TV administrators to review the question and respond appropriately.

In some cases, general versions of submitted questions may be used for educational purposes in lectures or articles. However, personal details will never be publicly disclosed without consent.

4. Purpose of Information Collection

Information collected through the website is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing Islamic guidance and answering submitted questions
  • Improving the educational content offered through Badawy TV
  • Maintaining communication with individuals seeking assistance
  • Managing the functionality and security of the website

Badawy TV does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

Badawy TV takes appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect user information. While the internet cannot guarantee absolute security, reasonable steps are taken to ensure that submitted information remains protected.

6. Third Party Services

Our website may contain links to external services such as:

  • YouTube
  • WhatsApp
  • Social media platforms

These platforms operate independently and maintain their own privacy policies.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Badawy TV does not intentionally track visitors through invasive tracking technologies. Basic technical information such as browser type or page visits may be recorded by the web hosting service for security and performance monitoring.

8. Children's Privacy

Badawy TV encourages parents to supervise children's use of online resources. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental guidance.

9. Updates to Privacy Policy

Badawy TV may update this Privacy Policy periodically in order to reflect improvements to the website or changes in legal requirements.

10. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy you may contact Badawy TV through the contact section of the website.
