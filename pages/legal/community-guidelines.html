Badawy TV Community Guidelines

Badawy TV is built to promote respectful Islamic learning and discussion.

Respectful Communication

All users must communicate respectfully. Insults, harassment, hate speech, or abusive language will not be tolerated.

Authentic Questions

The Ask Imam section is intended for sincere religious questions related to Islamic teachings.

Prohibited Content

  • Hate speech
  • Harassment
  • Spam or advertising
  • False religious claims
  • Content promoting violence

Scholarly Respect

Users must respect scholars, teachers, and fellow community members.

Moderation

Badawy TV reserves the right to remove content or restrict access to users who violate these guidelines.

Community Mission

Our mission is to promote knowledge, unity, and respectful dialogue within the global Muslim community.
