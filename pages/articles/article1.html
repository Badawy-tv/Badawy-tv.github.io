Tawheed – The Oneness of Allah

Tawheed is the foundation of Islam. It means believing that Allah is One without partners and that He alone deserves worship.

Every prophet came with the same message: worship Allah alone and avoid false gods.

Understanding Tawheed strengthens faith and protects a Muslim from shirk.
