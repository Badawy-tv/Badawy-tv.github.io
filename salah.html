How to Pray Correctly (Salah)

Salah is the second pillar of Islam and was made obligatory during the Night Journey (Isra and Mi’raj). Muslims pray five times daily: Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha.

Conditions of Prayer

  • Purification (Wudu)
  • Facing the Qiblah
  • Covering the Awrah
  • Intention (Niyyah)

Key Elements

Every unit (rak‘ah) must include recitation of Surah Al-Fatihah. Proper prayer includes humility (khushu’) and following the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.
